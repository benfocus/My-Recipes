Rib Roast

This is a great recipe for the holidays.

Ingredients

  • 1 (3-4 pound) bone-in rib roast
  • 1 tablespoon kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup dry red wine
  • 1 cup beef broth

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. Season the roast with salt and pepper.
  3. Heat the olive oil in a large roasting pan over medium-high heat.
  4. Sear the roast on all sides until browned.
  5. Remove the roast from the pan and set aside..
  6. Add the onion and garlic to the pan and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  7. Add the red wine and beef broth to the pan and bring to a boil.
  8. Return the roast to the pan and roast for 2-2 1/2 hours, or until a meat thermometer inserted into the thickest part of the roast registers 120 degrees F (50 degrees C).
  9. Remove the roast from the oven and let it rest for 15 minutes before carving.
