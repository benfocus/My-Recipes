Sesame Chicken

Enjoy this recipe when you are feeling creative.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1/2 cup cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup sesame seeds
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes

Instructions

  1. In a medium bowl, combine the flour, salt, and pepper.
  2. In a shallow dish, beat the egg.
  3. In a separate shallow dish, combine the cornstarch and sesame seeds.
  4. Dredge the chicken pieces in the flour mixture, then dip in the egg, and then coat in the cornstarch mixture.
  5. Heat the oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  6. Cook the chicken in batches for 2-3 minutes per side, or until golden brown and cooked through.
  7. While the chicken is cooking, make the sauce by combining the soy sauce, rice vinegar, honey, sesame oil, ginger, and red pepper flakes in a small bowl.
  8. Add the sauce to the skillet and bring to a boil.
  9. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 1-2 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened.
  10. Return the chicken to the skillet and toss to coat in the sauce.
  11. Serve immediately with rice or noodles.
