Lasagna

This is the best recipe for lasagna especially if you are hungry and love an original taste.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 (15 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 (15 ounce) can tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 (15 ounce) container ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese/li>

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat. Drain off any excess fat.
  3. Add the onion and garlic to the skillet and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  4. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Bring to a simmer and cook for 15 minutes, or until thickened.
  5. In a medium bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, egg, and 1/2 cup of the mozzarella cheese.
  6. Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  7. Top with 3 lasagna noodles, overlapping as needed.
  8. Spread half of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles.
  9. Top with half of the remaining meat sauce.
  10. Repeat layers.
  11. Top with the remaining mozzarella cheese and Parmesan cheese.
  12. Bake for 30 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  13. Let stand for 10 minutes before serving.
